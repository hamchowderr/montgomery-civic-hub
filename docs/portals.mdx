## Portal Overview

Each portal targets a specific audience with tailored AI personality, data focus, and visualizations.

| Portal | Route | Audience | Focus Areas |
|--------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Resident | `/resident` | Citizens | Safety, services, neighborhoods |
| Business | `/business` | Business owners | Permits, licenses, economic data |
| City Staff | `/citystaff` | Government staff | Infrastructure, budgets, operations |
| Researcher | `/researcher` | Academics | Crime trends, demographics, analysis |

## Portal Isolation

Each portal maintains full isolation:

- **System prompts** — Tailored tone, focus areas, and formatting per portal (defined in `lib/ai/prompts.ts`)
- **Data queries** — Each portal queries different ArcGIS datasets relevant to its audience
- **UI components** — Colocated under each portal's route group
- **Onboarding tours** — 6-7 step guided tours specific to each portal's features

## Layout

Portals use a resizable two-panel layout:
- **Left panel**: AI chat (collapsible)
- **Right panel**: Tabbed data view (Map / Table / Chart)

Year range filtering is available across all portals, letting users explore data from 2018 to present.
