## Who Is This For?

City leadership, department heads, the mayor's office, and senior officials who need a high-level operational view across all city services. The Executive Portal is a scrollable KPI command center designed for decision makers who need to see the big picture at a glance.

## Dashboard Sections

The Executive Portal is a single scrollable page with five key sections:

### Morning Briefing Generator

Start your day with an AI-generated briefing that summarizes the state of city operations:

- **Quick briefing** — A concise overview with top-line metrics and urgent items
- **Detailed briefing** — A comprehensive report covering all departments, trends, and recommended actions

The AI pulls from all city datasets to generate briefings that are ready for morning meetings.

### KPI Grid

A grid of key performance indicators with **sparkline charts** showing trends at a glance:

- 311 request volumes and resolution rates
- Code violation counts and clearance rates
- Permit activity and processing times
- Infrastructure project status
- Public safety metrics

Each KPI card shows the current value, trend direction, and a sparkline for quick visual assessment.

### Alert Priority Queue

A prioritized list of items that need executive attention:

- High-volume 311 categories
- Districts with unusual activity spikes
- Staffing alerts and vacancy thresholds
- Infrastructure milestones and deadlines

Alerts are ranked by urgency so you can focus on what matters most.

### Cross-Portal Pulse Cards

Summary cards pulling key metrics from each of the other portals:

- **Resident pulse** — 311 trends, community service usage
- **Business pulse** — Permit and license activity
- **City Staff pulse** — Infrastructure and enforcement status
- **Researcher pulse** — Data quality and coverage metrics

### District Comparison Matrix

A side-by-side comparison of all 9 council districts across multiple service metrics. Quickly identify which districts are over- or under-served relative to others.

## What the AI Can Do

The Executive Portal AI assistant:
- Generates **morning briefings** (quick or detailed) summarizing city operations across all datasets
- Highlights **priority alerts** that need immediate attention
- Provides **cross-portal intelligence** — connecting data points from resident, business, staff, and researcher perspectives
- Compares **district performance** with data-backed metrics
- Structures responses for **executive consumption** — headlines first, details on demand

### AI Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|---|---|
| `generate_briefing` | Generates a quick or detailed morning briefing from all city data |
| `highlight_alert` | Scrolls to and highlights a specific priority alert in the queue |
| `switch_executive_view` | Navigates to a specific section of the executive dashboard |

<Tip>
Start each morning with "Generate a morning briefing" to get a concise summary of what happened overnight and what needs your attention today.
</Tip>
