## Who Is This For?

Montgomery city staff — department heads, analysts, planners, and officials who need decision-ready data for the mayor, city council, planning department, or housing authority.

## Available Datasets

| Dataset | What It Shows | Updated |
|---|---|---|
| Paving Projects | Street resurfacing — status, location, contractor, mileage | As projects progress |
| Code Violations | Enforcement cases — type, status, lien status | Real-time |
| 311 Service Requests | Departmental workload — requests by type, status, district | Real-time |
| Pavement Assessment 2025 | Road condition ratings and quality scores | Annual |
| City-Owned Properties | Municipal property inventory for management | Periodically |
| Nuisance Reports | Nuisance complaints and demolitions | Real-time |

## Map Layers

The City Staff Portal map shows:
- **Paving Projects** — Color-coded by status and completion year
- **Code Violations** — Violation locations by type
- **311 Service Requests** — Request density and status
- **Council Districts** — Toggleable boundary overlay

## Executive Briefing Mode

When you ask for a "report," "briefing," or "summary for the mayor," the AI structures its response as:

1. **Key Finding** — One-sentence headline
2. **Data Points** — 3-5 specific metrics with sources
3. **Trend** — Direction and magnitude of change
4. **Recommendation** — What the data suggests the city should consider
5. **Data Sources** — Which datasets were used

<Tip>
The City Staff AI cross-references datasets automatically. Ask it to correlate 311 requests with code violations, or overlay paving projects with pavement assessment scores.
</Tip>

## What the AI Can Do

The City Staff Portal AI assistant:
- Provides **analytical, report-oriented** responses ready for presentations
- Cites **precise field values, counts, percentages, and trends**
- Flags **data limitations and caveats** that could affect decisions
- **Cross-references datasets** for interdisciplinary insights
- Incorporates **public safety context** (MPD staffing, 911 center vacancies)
- Structures data for **easy inclusion in reports**
