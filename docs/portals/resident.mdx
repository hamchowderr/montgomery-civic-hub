## Who Is This For?

Montgomery residents who want to understand what's happening in their neighborhood. Whether you're checking on a 311 request, looking up flood zones, or finding your nearest community center, the Resident Portal puts city data at your fingertips.

## Available Datasets

| Dataset | What It Shows | Updated |
|---|---|---|
| 311 Service Requests | Citizen-reported issues — potholes, streetlights, trash, drainage | Real-time |
| Health Care Facilities | Hospitals, clinics, health centers | As locations change |
| Recycling Locations | Drop-off recycling sites | Periodically |
| Garbage Collection Schedule | Trash pickup zones and schedules | Seasonal |
| Community Centers | Public community center locations | As locations change |
| Libraries | Public library branches | As locations change |
| Police Facilities | Police stations and substations | As locations change |
| Fire Stations | Fire department locations | As locations change |
| Tornado Sirens | Emergency siren locations | As locations change |
| Code Violations | Property code violations in your area | Real-time |
| Flood Hazard Areas | FEMA flood zone boundaries | Periodically |

## Map Layers

The Resident Portal map shows:
- **311 Service Requests** — Color-coded by status (open, in progress, closed)
- **Police & Fire Facilities** — Station locations across the city
- **Community Centers & Libraries** — Public resource locations
- **Council Districts** — Toggleable boundary overlay with 9 color-coded districts

## What the AI Can Do

The Resident Portal AI assistant:
- Queries **real city data** from Montgomery's ArcGIS systems
- Explains data in **plain, friendly language** — no technical jargon
- Suggests **practical next steps** (how to file a 311 request, contact your council member)
- Connects current data to **Montgomery's history** and civic context
- Proactively mentions **related datasets** you might not know about
- Can **control the dashboard** — switching map layers, changing date filters, toggling views

<Tip>
The AI keeps responses short (2-3 sentences) and always suggests what you can do next. If you need more detail, just ask a follow-up question.
</Tip>
