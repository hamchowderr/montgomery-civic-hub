## Who Is This For?

Business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, and anyone exploring economic opportunities in Montgomery. The Business Portal provides data-driven intelligence about permits, licenses, commercial zones, and development opportunities.

## Available Datasets

| Dataset | What It Shows | Updated |
|---|---|---|
| Business Licenses | Active registrations, license types, DBA names, locations | As issued/renewed |
| Construction Permits | Building, electrical, mechanical, plumbing permits | As issued |
| Entertainment Districts | Designated entertainment zone boundaries | Periodically |
| Zoning | Land use zoning classifications | Periodically |
| City-Owned Properties | Municipal property inventory (potential development sites) | Periodically |
| Code Violations | Property code violations (neighborhood conditions) | Real-time |

## Map Layers

The Business Portal map shows:
- **Business Licenses** — License locations across Montgomery
- **Construction Permits** — Active permit locations by type
- **Entertainment Districts** — Zone boundaries
- **Council Districts** — Toggleable boundary overlay

## What the AI Can Do

The Business Portal AI assistant:
- Cites **specific permit counts, license statistics, and location data**
- Frames data around **business decisions and economic opportunity**
- Highlights **growth areas and emerging development zones**
- Provides context on Montgomery's **data center economy** (Meta's $1.5B campus, DC BLOX)
- Identifies **potential development sites** using city-owned property and zoning data
- Connects city data to **investment and expansion decisions**

<Note>
Montgomery's economy is anchored by government (state capital), Hyundai Motor Manufacturing, Maxwell Air Force Base, and a rapidly growing data center sector. The AI provides economic context alongside raw data.
</Note>
