Montgomery Civic Hub is a six-portal civic dashboard that puts the power of Montgomery's open data into the hands of the people who need it most. Powered by AI and built on 40+ city datasets, it transforms raw government data into actionable insights.

## Why Montgomery Civic Hub?

Montgomery, Alabama — the state capital and birthplace of the modern civil rights movement — is a city of **~200,000 residents** across **9 council districts**. The city generates vast amounts of public data: 311 service requests, building permits, business licenses, code violations, infrastructure projects, and more.

Most of this data sits in technical systems that everyday people can't easily access. The Civic Hub changes that by providing a conversational AI interface backed by real city data.

## Every Portal Includes

- **AI Chat** — Ask questions in plain English, get answers backed by real city data
- **Interactive Map** — Visualize data points, boundaries, and overlays on a live map
- **Data Tables** — Sort, filter, and browse raw datasets
- **Charts** — See trends and patterns over time
- **Year Filter** — Focus on any date range from 2018 to present
- **Portal-Specific Sub-Pages** — Dedicated views for specialized data (newsfeeds, dashboards, explorers, heritage layers, and more)
- **AI Dashboard Control** — The AI can switch tabs, toggle layers, navigate sub-pages, generate reports, and control UI elements on your behalf
